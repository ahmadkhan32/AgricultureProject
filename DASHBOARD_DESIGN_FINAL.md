- Consistent `space-y-6` or `space-y-8`

- Descriptions: `text-emerald-50` or `text-gray-600`
- Body: Standard sizes with proper hierarchy

### Borders & Shadows:
- Cards: `rounded-xl shadow-lg border border-gray-100`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-xl`
- Buttons: `rounded-xl shadow-lg`
